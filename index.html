About

I am Anurag Pisupati, an Associate Professor at ESTACA in Laval, France, working in mechanical engineering, composite materials, and high-performance material systems.

My work focuses on composite processing, process modeling, damage mechanisms, aging, recycling, and the development of bio-based and functional composites.

Experience

Associate Professor

ESTACA, Laval, France · 2025–Present

Teaching mechanical engineering subjects and supervising student projects. Research focused on high-performance materials, damage mechanisms, aging, recycling, and multiphysics problems.

Research and Innovation Project Manager

Technical Center for Plastics and Composites, Laval, France · 2022–2025

Managed national and international research projects in composites, recycling, smart materials, and process optimization. Developed collaborative projects, including a Horizon Europe funded project, and led contract R&D projects for industrial partners.

Research Engineer

Armines, Douai, France · 2020–2022

Worked on composite waste recovery, valorization of glass fiber waste, and development of bio-based hybrid composites.
