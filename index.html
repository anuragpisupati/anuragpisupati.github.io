About

I am Anurag Pisupati, an Associate Professor at ESTACA in Laval, France, working in mechanical engineering, composite materials, and high-performance material systems.

My work focuses on composite processing, process modeling, damage mechanisms, aging, recycling, and the development of bio-based and functional composites. I have experience connecting experimental characterization, simulation, and applied research to support sustainable and advanced composite technologies.

Alongside research, I teach mechanical engineering subjects and supervise student projects, with a strong interest in helping students connect theory, experimentation, and engineering applications.
